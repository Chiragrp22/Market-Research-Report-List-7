<p><p>The scooter tire market comprises two primary types: solid tires and pneumatic tires. Solid tires are constructed from a dense rubber material, offering durability and maintenance-free performance, ideal for urban environments and frequent short trips. In contrast, pneumatic tires are air-filled, providing better shock absorption and enhanced grip on various surfaces, which improves comfort during longer rides. Each type has its advantages, catering to different rider preferences and usage scenarios, influencing their selection based on performance needs and riding conditions.</p></p>
