<p><p>Electromagnetic diaphragm metering pumps are categorized into two main types based on control mechanisms: manual control and automatic control. Manual control pumps require human input to adjust flow rates and timing, allowing for precise, user-driven operations. In contrast, automatic control pumps utilize sensors and control systems to regulate flow rates continuously and adaptively, ensuring consistent performance in various applications. Both types are essential for precise dosing and fluid management in industries such as water treatment, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
