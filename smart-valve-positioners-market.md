<p><ul><li>Single Acting Positioners</li><li>Double Acting Positioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart valve positioners market is typically segmented into single-acting and double-acting positioners. Single-acting positioners use pneumatic pressure in one direction, making them energy-efficient and suitable for specific applications where minimal force is required. In contrast, double-acting positioners utilize pressure in both directions, offering more control and robustness for demanding applications. Both types enhance the precision and responsiveness of valve systems, crucial in industries like oil, gas, and chemical processing, where optimal flow control is essential.</p></p>
