<p><p>Heated humidifier devices are categorized into two primary market types: stand-alone and integrated. Stand-alone humidifiers operate independently, providing targeted humidity control in specific areas or rooms. They are often portable and flexible, catering to individual needs. In contrast, integrated humidifiers are built into HVAC systems, delivering humidity control throughout an entire building efficiently. They offer seamless operation and maintenance, making them suitable for larger spaces, while enhancing overall indoor air quality and comfort.</p></p>
