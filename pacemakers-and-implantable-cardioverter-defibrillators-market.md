<p><p>The pacemakers and implantable cardioverter defibrillators market comprises two primary segments. Pacemakers are devices implanted in patients to regulate heartbeats, addressing bradycardia and other arrhythmias. They help maintain adequate heart rhythm through electrical impulses. In contrast, implantable cardioverter defibrillators (ICDs) are designed to monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks when life-threatening arrhythmias occur, preventing sudden cardiac arrest. Both devices play vital roles in managing cardiovascular conditions, enhancing patient quality of life, and reducing mortality risks.</p></p>
