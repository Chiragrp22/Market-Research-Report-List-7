<p><strong>The Electrostatic Chuck for CVD Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrostatic chucks (ESCs) are critical components in Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) equipment, used to hold substrates in place during the deposition process. The Coulomb-type ESC employs electrostatic forces generated by applied voltage to grip the substrate securely. In contrast, the Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type relies on a combination of electrostatic attraction and induced charges on the substrate to achieve adhesion. Both types enhance process stability and uniformity by ensuring precise substrate positioning during the CVD process.</p></p>
