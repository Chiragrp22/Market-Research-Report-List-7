<p><strong>The Subsurface Safety Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ground Control Safety Valves</li><li>Subsurface Control Safety Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsurface safety valves market primarily includes two types: Ground Control Safety Valves and Subsurface Control Safety Valves. Ground Control Safety Valves are activated from the surface, offering a reliable way to control well flow during emergencies. In contrast, Subsurface Control Safety Valves are installed downhole and automatically seal the well in case of a pressure surge or failure, ensuring safety and integrity. Both types are essential for preventing uncontrolled production and protecting personnel and the environment.</p></p>
