<p><strong>The Spinal Cage Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyetheretherketone</li><li>Titanium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal cage market is primarily divided into two types: polyetheretherketone (PEEK) and titanium. PEEK spinal cages are favored for their biocompatibility, lightweight nature, and ability to mimic the mechanical properties of bone, making them a popular choice for spinal fusion procedures. On the other hand, titanium spinal cages are known for their strength, durability, and radiopacity, which aids in post-operative imaging. Both materials offer unique advantages, catering to varying surgical needs and patient preferences in spinal surgery.</p></p>
