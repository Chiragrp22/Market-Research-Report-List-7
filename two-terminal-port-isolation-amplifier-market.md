<p><ul><li>Input Isolation</li><li>Output Isolation</li><li>Bidirectional Isolation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-terminal port isolation amplifier market encompasses three main types: input isolation, output isolation, and bidirectional isolation. Input isolation amplifiers prevent ground loops and ensure signal integrity by isolating the input signal from the ground. Output isolation amplifiers similarly isolate the output from the system ground, enhancing overall stability and reliability. Bidirectional isolation amplifiers allow for signal transfer in both directions while maintaining electrical separation, making them ideal for applications requiring simultaneous data flow and isolation in various systems.</p></p>
