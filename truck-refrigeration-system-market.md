<p><strong>The Truck Refrigeration System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split System</li><li>Roof Mount System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck refrigeration system market includes two main types: Split System and Roof Mount System. The Split System separates the compressor and condenser, typically mounted on the chassis, from the evaporator inside the cargo area, offering flexibility and efficient cooling for larger trucks. The Roof Mount System integrates all components on the truck's roof, ideal for smaller vehicles with space constraints. Both systems ensure temperature control for perishable goods, catering to diverse transportation needs in the logistics and food industries.</p></p>
