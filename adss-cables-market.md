<p><strong>The ADSS Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ADSS Flextube</li><li>ADSS Loose Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ADSS cables, or All-Dielectric Self-Supporting cables, are categorized mainly into two types: ADSS Flextube and ADSS Loose Tube. ADSS Flextube cables feature a compact design with flexible tubes that accommodate various fiber counts, making them suitable for tight spaces and urban installations. In contrast, ADSS Loose Tube cables consist of individual optical fibers housed in separated tubes, providing enhanced protection against environmental factors and accommodating expansion and contraction due to temperature fluctuations. Both types are essential for reliable telecommunications over longer distances.</p></p>
