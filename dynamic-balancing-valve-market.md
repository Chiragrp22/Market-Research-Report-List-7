<p><p>The dynamic balancing valve market comprises manual and automatic balancing valves, each serving distinct applications. Manual balancing valves require manual adjustment to optimize flow rates within a system, providing precise control but needing regular monitoring. In contrast, automatic balancing valves adjust flow rates autonomously, ensuring consistent performance across varying conditions without human intervention. This automation enhances system efficiency and reduces energy consumption, making automatic valves increasingly popular in modern HVAC systems, while manual valves continue to be favored for specific traditional applications.</p></p>
