<p><p>The biaxial geogrid market encompasses three main types: extruded, knitted/woven, and bonded. Extruded geogrids are formed by melting and shaping plastic to create strong, tensile grids, ideal for stabilization applications. Knitted/woven geogrids utilize interlaced fibers for enhanced flexibility and strength, commonly used in construction to support soil and reinforce materials. Bonded geogrids are created by adhering layers of geotextile fabrics, providing robust reinforcement and effective load distribution, particularly in retaining wall and pavement projects. Each type serves distinct engineering needs.</p></p>
