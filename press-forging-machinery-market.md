<p><p>The press forging machinery market includes various types of processes essential for shaping metal. Closed die forging involves shaping heated metal within a mold, creating precise and intricate parts. Open die forging uses flat or simple dies, allowing for larger shapes but with less precision. Extrusion forces metal through a die to produce long shapes, like rods or tubes. Other methods might include hybrid techniques or specialized applications, catering to diverse industrial needs and enhancing production capabilities.</p></p>
