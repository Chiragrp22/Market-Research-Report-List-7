<p><p>The ceramic ultrafiltration membrane market is primarily segmented into two types: flat-sheet membranes and pipe membranes. Flat-sheet membranes feature a planar design, offering effective filtration in various applications, such as water treatment and wastewater management. In contrast, pipe membranes are cylindrical in shape, providing a compact solution ideal for high-throughput processes and reducing space requirements. Both types leverage the durability and chemical resistance of ceramics, making them suitable for demanding environments in industries ranging from food processing to pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
