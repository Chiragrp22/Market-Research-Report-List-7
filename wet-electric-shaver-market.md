<p><p>The wet electric shaver market comprises two main types: rotary shavers and reciprocating shavers. Rotary shavers feature circular blades that move in a rotating motion, adapting to facial contours for a close shave. They are ideal for those with thicker hair and provide a smooth finish. In contrast, reciprocating shavers use oscillating blades that move back and forth, working well for fine hair and often providing versatility in trimming facial hair. Both types cater to different shaving preferences and needs.</p></p>
