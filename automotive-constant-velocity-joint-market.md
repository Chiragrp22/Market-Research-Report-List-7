<p><p>The Automotive Constant Velocity Joint (CV Joint) market is segmented into outboard and inboard joints. Outboard joints are typically located at the wheel end of the axle and are crucial for allowing wheels to turn while delivering power from the engine. Inboard joints, found closer to the transmission, facilitate smooth power transfer while accommodating changes in axle length. Both joint types are essential for ensuring optimal vehicle performance, handling, and stability during diverse driving conditions.</p></p>
