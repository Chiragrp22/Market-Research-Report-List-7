<p><p>The Active IR Sensors market includes two primary types: Thermal Active IR Sensors and Quantum Active IR Sensors. Thermal Active IR Sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by objects, translating heat variations into signal changes for applications in surveillance and temperature measurement. Quantum Active IR Sensors, on the other hand, utilize semiconductor materials to detect infrared light through photon interactions, enabling highly sensitive measurements in areas such as gas detection and biomedical applications. Both types cater to diverse industries needing precise thermal and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
