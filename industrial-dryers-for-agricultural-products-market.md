<p><p>The industrial dryers for agricultural products consist mainly of hot air drying equipment and heated surface drying equipment. Hot air drying uses heated air to evaporate moisture from crops, effectively preserving their quality and extending shelf life. Heated surface drying utilizes heated surfaces to transfer heat directly to the product, facilitating moisture removal. Both methods are essential for enhancing food storage, maintaining nutritional values, and enabling longer market reach for various agricultural outputs. This optimization supports the agricultural supply chain efficiency.</p></p>
