<p><p>The absorbable bone fixation device market is categorized into two main types: synthetic and natural materials. Synthetic materials, such as polylactic acid and polyglycolic acid, offer customizable properties, allowing for tailored mechanical strength and degradation rates. In contrast, natural materials, derived from biological sources like collagen or chitosan, provide biocompatibility and promote natural healing. Both types are designed to stabilize fractures and support bone regeneration while eventually being absorbed by the body, reducing the need for additional surgeries.</p></p>
