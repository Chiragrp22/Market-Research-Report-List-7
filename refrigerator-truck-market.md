<p><p>Refrigerator trucks, also known as reefer trucks, are specialized vehicles designed to transport perishable goods at controlled temperatures. The **Single Temperature Type** maintains a uniform temperature throughout the cargo area, ideal for transporting a single type of product, such as frozen foods or fresh produce. The **Multi-Temperature Type** features separate compartments with different temperature settings, allowing the simultaneous transport of diverse goods, like frozen, chilled, and ambient items, catering to varied logistics needs efficiently. Both types ensure product freshness and quality during transit.</p></p>
