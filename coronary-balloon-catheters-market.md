<p><p>Coronary balloon catheters are medical devices used primarily in percutaneous coronary interventions (PCI) to treat narrowed or blocked coronary arteries. These catheters are equipped with a balloon that is inflated at the site of blockage, allowing for the dilatation of the artery, improving blood flow. The market for coronary balloon catheters is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing prevalence of coronary artery disease, fueled by lifestyle factors such as obesity and diabetes.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in technology, including the development of drug-eluting balloons, have enhanced treatment efficacy and patient outcomes, contributing to the market's expansion. The Coronary Balloon Catheters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period. Favorable reimbursement policies, rising awareness of minimally invasive procedures, and a growing elderly population are further factors driving demand. Recent trends indicate a shift towards the use of bioresorbable materials in balloon catheter designs and an increase in the adoption of combination therapies to improve procedural outcomes.
