<p><p>The PETG sheet market comprises three primary types: Extruded Grade, Injection Molding Grade, and Blow Molding Grade PETG. Extruded Grade PETG is ideal for applications requiring excellent clarity and impact resistance, commonly used in displays and signage. Injection Molding Grade PETG is designed for precision parts, offering high strength and flexibility for various molded applications. Blow Molding Grade PETG focuses on creating hollow shapes, perfect for packaging and containers, due to its superior processing characteristics and durability.</p></p>
