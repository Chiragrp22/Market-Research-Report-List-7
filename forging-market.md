<p><strong>The Forging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Die Forgings</li><li>Open Die Forgings</li><li>Rolled Rings Forgings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The forging market is categorized into three main types: closed die forge, open die forge, and rolled ring forge. Closed die forging involves shaping metal within a die, resulting in precise dimensions and complex shapes, ideal for high-volume production. Open die forging, conversely, uses flat dies, allowing for larger, simpler parts with flexibility in shape. Rolled ring forging produces circular components by rolling heated metal, offering uniform thickness and strength, commonly used in applications like bearings and gears.</p></p>
