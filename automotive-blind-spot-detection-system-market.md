<p><p>The automotive blind spot detection system market comprises several types of sensors. Radar sensors utilize radio waves to detect nearby vehicles, providing reliable and long-range monitoring. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to identify objects in blind spots at shorter ranges, making them effective for low-speed scenarios. LIDAR sensors employ laser technology for high-resolution mapping of surroundings, offering precise detection of objects. Other technologies may include camera-based systems and fusion sensors, enhancing safety by reducing collision risks during lane changes.</p></p>
