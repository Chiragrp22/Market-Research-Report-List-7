Overall, the PMS market is poised for steady growth, driven by technological advancements and evolving automotive industry demands.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Pure Rubber Mounting</li><li>Hydraulic Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Powertrain Mounting System (PMS) market includes Pure Rubber Mounting and Hydraulic Mounting systems. Pure Rubber Mounting uses rubber components to absorb vibrations and shocks, offering cost-effectiveness and durability for lighter vehicles. Hydraulic Mounting incorporates fluid-filled chambers to provide superior vibration damping, ideal for high-performance or heavier vehicles requiring enhanced noise and vibration control. Both systems aim to stabilize the powertrain, improve ride comfort, and extend vehicle lifespan, catering to different automotive needs based on performance, weight, and cost considerations.</p></p>
