<p><strong>The Impregnated Graphite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin Impregnated</li><li>Metal Impregnated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The impregnated graphite market consists of two primary types: resin-impregnated and metal-impregnated graphite. Resin-impregnated graphite is treated with resin to enhance mechanical properties and corrosion resistance, making it suitable for applications in electrical components and heat exchangers. In contrast, metal-impregnated graphite incorporates metals to improve thermal conductivity and reduce wear, commonly used in lubricants and high-performance seals. Both types leverage the unique properties of graphite, catering to diverse industrial needs across sectors such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
