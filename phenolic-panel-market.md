<p><ul><li>Sandwich Panel</li><li>Ordinary Panel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phenolic panel market offers two main types: sandwich panels and ordinary panels. Sandwich panels are composed of a core layer sandwiched between two outer layers, providing enhanced insulation and structural integrity, making them ideal for applications in construction and cold storage. Ordinary panels, on the other hand, consist of a single sheet of phenolic material, offering basic insulation and chemical resistance for less demanding uses. Both types cater to various industries, including construction, transportation, and industrial applications.</p></p>
