<p><ul><li>Single Coil Spring Seal</li><li>Multiple Spring Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pusher seal markets offer two primary types: single coil spring seals and multiple spring seals. Single coil spring seals utilize a single spring to maintain a constant axial load, providing reliable sealing in various applications with moderate pressure. In contrast, multiple spring seals employ several springs, distributing the load more evenly and enhancing sealing performance, especially in high-pressure environments. Both types are essential in preventing leaks in rotating equipment and maintaining efficiency across industrial processes.</p></p>
