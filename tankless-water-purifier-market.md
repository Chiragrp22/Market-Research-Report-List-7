<p><strong>The Tankless Water Purifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RO Water Purifier</li><li>UV Water Purifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tankless water purifier market includes two primary types: RO (Reverse Osmosis) water purifiers and UV (Ultraviolet) water purifiers. RO water purifiers use a semi-permeable membrane to remove dissolved impurities and contaminants, ensuring high-quality purified water suitable for drinking. In contrast, UV water purifiers utilize ultraviolet light to kill bacteria and viruses, providing effective disinfection without chemicals. Both systems offer compact designs, providing instant water purification without the need for bulky storage tanks, ideal for modern, space-efficient living.</p></p>
