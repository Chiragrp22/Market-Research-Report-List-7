<p><strong>The Glycosidases Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Endoglycosidase</li><li>Exoglycosidase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glycosidases market comprises two primary types: endoglycosidases and exoglycosidases. Endoglycosidases act on internal glycosidic bonds within polysaccharides, facilitating the breakdown of complex carbohydrates into smaller oligosaccharides. In contrast, exoglycosidases cleave terminal glycosidic bonds, progressively releasing monosaccharides from the ends of carbohydrate chains. Together, these enzymes are crucial in various applications, including biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, and food industries, where they play a vital role in carbohydrate metabolism, degradation, and modification processes.</p></p>
