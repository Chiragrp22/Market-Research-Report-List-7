<p><ul><li>Sheet Based Membranes</li><li>Liquid Applied Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterproofing membranes are essential for preventing water infiltration in structures. **Sheet-based membranes** are pre-manufactured rolls or panels made from materials like bitumen, PVC, or EPDM. They are applied by unrolling and adhering to surfaces, offering durability and quick installation. **Liquid-applied membranes** are fluid coatings, such as polyurethane or acrylic, sprayed or brushed onto surfaces, forming a seamless, flexible layer. They adapt well to complex shapes and provide excellent adhesion. Both types cater to diverse construction needs, ensuring long-term water resistance and structural protection.</p></p>
