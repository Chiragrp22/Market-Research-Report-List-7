<p><p>The hybrid valve market comprises various materials, each offering unique advantages. Steel valves are durable and cost-effective, making them common in general applications. Tungsten carbide valves provide excellent wear resistance and are ideal for high-abrasion environments. Alloy valves combine multiple metals for enhanced performance and corrosion resistance. Titanium valves are lightweight and highly resistant to corrosive substances, suitable for harsh conditions. Duplex nickel valves offer superior strength and resistance to pitting and stress corrosion, making them versatile for demanding industrial applications.</p></p>
