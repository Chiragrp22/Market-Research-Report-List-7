<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Starter Market can be categorized into two main types: Direct Control and Electromagnetic Control. Direct Control starters rely on a mechanical link between the ignition and starter motor, providing immediate and responsive engagement. In contrast, Electromagnetic Control starters utilize an electromagnetic solenoid to engage the starter motor, offering advantages like reduced wear and improved reliability. Both types cater to diverse vehicle needs, balancing performance, efficiency, and usability in the demanding environment of commercial transportation.</p></p>
