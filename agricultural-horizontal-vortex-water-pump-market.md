<p><p>The Agricultural Horizontal Vortex Water Pump market is divided into two main types: Closed Vortex Pumps and Open Vortex Pumps. Closed Vortex Pumps are designed to handle liquids with solid particles efficiently, providing high suction lift and reduced turbulence, making them suitable for various agricultural applications. In contrast, Open Vortex Pumps feature a more exposed impeller design, allowing for a better handling of larger debris but may have lower efficiency in specific scenarios. Both types cater to diverse agricultural water management needs.</p></p>
