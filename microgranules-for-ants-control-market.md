<p><p>Microgranules for ant control are available in three primary application types: scattering, spray, and watering. Scattering involves distributing granules across affected areas, targeting foraging ants effectively. The spray method utilizes a fine mist of microgranule solutions to cover larger areas and penetrate nests. Watering involves dissolving microgranules in water, allowing for deep penetration into the soil and targeting hidden ant colonies. Each method offers unique advantages, catering to different environments and infestation levels for comprehensive ant management.</p></p>
