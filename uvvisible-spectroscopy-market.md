<p><ul><li>Single-beam Systems</li><li>Dual-beam Systems</li><li>Array-based Systems</li><li>Handheld Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV/Visible Spectroscopy market comprises several system types, each with distinct features. Single-beam systems measure light intensity before and after sample analysis, often favored for simplicity. Dual-beam systems compare a sample against a reference, enhancing accuracy by reducing environmental interference. Array-based systems utilize multiple detectors for rapid analysis across various wavelengths simultaneously, ideal for high-throughput applications. Handheld systems offer portability and user-friendliness, catering to field applications and on-site measurements, making them popular in various industries.</p></p>
