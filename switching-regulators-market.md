<p><ul><li>Buck Regulators (Step-Down)</li><li>Boost Regulators (Step-Up)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The switching regulators market is primarily segmented into buck (step-down) and boost (step-up) regulators. Buck regulators efficiently convert higher input voltages to lower output voltages, making them ideal for applications like battery management and power supplies for electronic devices. Conversely, boost regulators increase voltage from a lower input, suitable for powering devices requiring higher voltage levels from smaller batteries. Both types are crucial in modern electronics, offering improved efficiency and minimizing heat generation, driving their widespread adoption across various industries.</p></p>
