<p><p>The optometry and eye exam equipment market includes various specialized devices essential for diagnosing and managing eye conditions. Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) provides high-resolution cross-sectional images of the retina. Fundus cameras capture detailed images of the interior of the eye. Perimeters measure visual field sensitivity, while topography maps the corneal surface. Lensmeters evaluate lens prescriptions, and chart projectors present visual acuity tests. Wavefront analyzers assess aberrations in the optical system, enhancing personalized eye care and treatment.</p></p>
