<p><p>The industrial dryers market encompasses various types tailored to specific drying needs. Rotary dryers utilize a rotating cylinder to evaporate moisture from materials, enhancing efficiency. Dispersion dryers promote rapid drying by dispersing material in a stream of hot air. Fluid bed dryers employ a bed of particles, suspended and heated by air, ensuring uniform drying. Belt dryers utilize conveyor belts to move products through a heated chamber. Other dryers include specialized equipment for unique applications, such as freeze or microwave dryers, catering to diverse industry requirements.</p></p>
