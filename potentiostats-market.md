In contrast, polypotentiostats can manage multiple electrodes at once, making them suitable for complex experiments involving arrays of electrodes or multiple working electrodes. Both types cater to growing demands in research, materials science, and analytical chemistry, enhancing precision and efficiency in electrochemical measurements across various industries.</p></p>
