<p><ul><li>AGM</li><li>EFB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TAXI battery market primarily features two types: AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and EFB (Enhanced Flooded Battery). AGM batteries are advanced, maintenance-free, and ideal for start-stop systems, offering high power output, durability, and vibration resistance. EFB batteries are an upgraded version of traditional flooded batteries, designed for basic start-stop functionality and cost-effectiveness. Both cater to modern taxi demands, with AGM suited for high-performance vehicles and EFB for budget-friendly options, ensuring reliable performance and longevity in frequent stop-start urban driving conditions.</p></p>
