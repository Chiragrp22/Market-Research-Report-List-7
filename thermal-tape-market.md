<p><p>The thermal tape market is primarily segmented into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided thermal tape features adhesive on one side, making it ideal for applications where heat dissipation or adhesive bonding is required on a single surface. In contrast, double-sided thermal tape has adhesive on both sides, allowing for effective attachment between two surfaces, enhancing thermal conductivity. Both types are crucial in electronics, automotive, and industrial applications, where efficient heat management and secure bonding are essential.</p></p>
