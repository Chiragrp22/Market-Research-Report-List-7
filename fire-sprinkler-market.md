<p><strong>The Fire Sprinkler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The fire sprinkler market includes several types designed for different applications: </p><p>- **Wet Fire Sprinklers** contain water in the pipes, activated by heat to release water directly.</p><p>- **Dry Fire Sprinklers** have pressurized air in the pipes and release water when heated, ideal for colder environments. </p><p>- **Deluge Fire Sprinklers** open simultaneously to flood an area with water, used in high-hazard settings. </p><p>- **Preaction Fire Sprinklers** combine both wet and dry systems, requiring dual activation for added safety in sensitive environments.</p></p>
