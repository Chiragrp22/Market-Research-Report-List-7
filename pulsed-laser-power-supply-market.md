<p><p>The Pulsed Laser Power Supply market is categorized into two types: Single Pulse and Multipulse systems. Single Pulse power supplies deliver a single, high-energy pulse, ideal for applications requiring high precision and minimal thermal impact, such as material ablation or medical procedures. In contrast, Multipulse systems generate a series of pulses over time, allowing for controlled energy delivery and improved processing efficiency in applications like machining and laser marking. Each type caters to different industrial and research needs, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
