<p><p>The car timing belts market can be categorized into two main types: trapezoidal tooth and arc tooth. Trapezoidal tooth belts feature a V-shaped tooth profile that provides efficient engagement with the pulleys, ensuring reliable power transmission and minimizing slippage. In contrast, arc tooth belts have a rounded tooth design that offers smoother operation and reduced noise levels. Both types cater to different engine designs, contributing to improved engine performance, longevity, and maintenance efficiency in vehicles.</p></p>
