<p><p>The specialty grade carbon black market consists of various types based on production methods and applications. Furnace black, produced through the incomplete combustion of hydrocarbons, is commonly used for tires and coatings. Channel black, made from burning oil or gas, offers high purity for electronic applications. Thermal black is derived from the thermal decomposition of hydrocarbons, providing excellent conductivity. Acetylene black, produced from acetylene gas, is known for its high structure and conductivity, making it suitable for specialty applications. Other types may include color and pigment blacks used in various industries.</p></p>
