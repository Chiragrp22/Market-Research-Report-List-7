<p><p>Tuned absorbers are designed to mitigate vibrations in structures. The market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Passive Absorbers**: Rely on fixed parameters to counteract vibrations without external energy, offering simplicity and reliability.</p><p>2. **Active Absorbers**: Utilize sensors and actuators to dynamically respond to changing vibration patterns, providing enhanced performance but requiring energy sources.</p><p>3. **Semi-Active Absorbers**: Combine passive and active features, employing adjustable mechanisms to adapt to varying conditions, striking a balance between effectiveness and energy efficiency.</p></p>
