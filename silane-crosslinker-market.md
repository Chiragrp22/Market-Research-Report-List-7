<p><p>The silane crosslinker market is categorized into several types based on the chemical modifications they undergo. Deacidified types eliminate acidic components to enhance stability and performance. Deketoxime types are designed to remove toxic byproducts during curing. Dealcoholized types reduce alcohol content for improved efficiency in crosslinking. Deamidated types eliminate amide groups to enhance compatibility with various substrates. Finally, deacetone types aim to reduce acetone presence, promoting safer handling and application in various industries such as construction and automotive.</p></p>
