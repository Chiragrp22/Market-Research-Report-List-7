<p><p>The Triple-clad Fiber market is segmented into Single-mode and Multi-mode Triple-clad Fibers. Single-mode Triple-clad Fiber allows the transmission of light through a single path, enhancing performance over long distances with minimal signal loss, making it ideal for telecommunications. In contrast, Multi-mode Triple-clad Fiber supports multiple light paths, enabling higher data capacity over shorter distances, suitable for data centers and local area networks. Both types leverage advanced cladding structures to improve efficiency and reduce attenuation in fiber optic applications.</p></p>
