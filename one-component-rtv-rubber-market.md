<p><ul><li>Addition Type</li><li>Condensation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The One-Component RTV (Room Temperature Vulcanizing) rubber market is divided into two main types: addition type and condensation type. Addition type RTVs cure through addition reactions, resulting in a fast-setting, odorless rubber with high purity and durability. In contrast, condensation type RTVs cure by releasing a small molecule, typically alcohol, leading to a slower setting process with a characteristic odor. Both types are widely used in molding, sealing, and adhesive applications due to their versatility and performance characteristics.</p></p>
