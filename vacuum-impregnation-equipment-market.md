<p><p>The vacuum impregnation equipment market is categorized into two main types: wet and dry. The wet market involves the use of liquid resins or adhesives to fill voids in porous materials, enhancing their strength and resistance to fluids. This method is widely used in industries like automotive and aerospace. In contrast, the dry market employs solid materials or powders to achieve impregnation, often benefiting applications requiring high thermal resistance and improved structural integrity. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
