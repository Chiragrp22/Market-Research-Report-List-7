<p><p>The SDS Adapter market primarily includes Chuck Adapters and Flexi-Click Adapters. Flexi-Click Adapters enhance versatility by offering multiple operational modes, allowing users to quickly switch between different functions, such as drilling and screwdriving. Both adapter types are essential for maximizing the utility and performance of power tools across various construction and DIY applications.</p></p>
