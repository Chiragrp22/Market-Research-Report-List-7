<p><p>The roadway dispenser market consists of two main types: manual and electric dispensers. Manual dispensers require human intervention for operation, typically utilizing hand pumps or gravity flow to dispense fluids like fuel or water. These are often simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, electric dispensers use electric power for automated dispensing, offering greater efficiency, precision, and faster service. They often feature digital displays and are more suitable for high-demand environments, enhancing user experience and operational convenience.</p></p>
