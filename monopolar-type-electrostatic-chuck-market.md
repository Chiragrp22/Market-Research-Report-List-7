<p><strong>The Monopolar Type Electrostatic Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Monopolar type electrostatic chucks (ESCs) are devices used to hold wafer substrates in semiconductor processing. The Coulomb type relies on electrostatic forces generated between charged plates and grounded substrates, ensuring strong adhesion. In contrast, the Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type utilizes a combination of electric field effects and dielectric properties, optimizing grip while minimizing charge buildup. Both types are crucial for enhancing precision and efficiency in manufacturing, catering to varying production needs in the semiconductor market.</p></p>
