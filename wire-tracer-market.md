<p><ul><li>Active Tracing</li><li>Passive Tracing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wire tracer market is primarily divided into two types: active tracing and passive tracing. Active tracing involves the use of tools that send signals through wires, allowing for precise location tracking and fault identification. It is ideal for complex wiring systems. In contrast, passive tracing relies on detecting existing signals or electromagnetic fields without interference. This method is less intrusive and can be used for simple diagnostics in various environments, making it suitable for routine maintenance and troubleshooting.</p></p>
