<p><p>Continuous Low Pressure Vacuum Carburizing Furnaces are categorized into three main types: Single Chamber, Double Chamber, and Multi Chamber. Single Chamber furnaces operate in a single enclosed space, optimizing space and simplicity. Double Chamber systems allow for the separation of carburizing and quenching processes, enhancing efficiency and control. Multi Chamber furnaces offer maximum flexibility by integrating multiple chambers, facilitating different processes simultaneously and improving throughput. Each type caters to varying production needs, offering distinct advantages in efficiency and operational control.</p></p>
