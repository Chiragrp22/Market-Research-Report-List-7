<p><p>The geophysical processing and imaging market encompasses various technologies used to investigate subsurface structures. Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) employs radar pulses to map underground features, ideal for archaeological and environmental assessments. Electromagnetic methods utilize electromagnetic fields to detect conductivity variations, suitable for mineral exploration and infrastructure health. LIDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) uses laser pulses for high-resolution terrain mapping, applicable in forestry, urban planning, and flood modeling. Other methods include seismic surveys and gravitational studies, each catering to specific geological and engineering needs.</p></p>
