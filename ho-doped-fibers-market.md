<p><p>Ho-doped fibers are categorized based on their cladding structures, which influence their performance in laser applications. Single-clad fibers have one layer of cladding, providing basic functionality. Double-clad fibers feature an additional cladding layer that enhances efficiency by allowing better pump light absorption, leading to higher output power. Triple-clad fibers add yet another layer, maximizing the light-guiding capability and improving overall performance. Each type serves different application needs, from telecommunications to medical and industrial lasers.</p></p>
