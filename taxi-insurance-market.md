<p><strong>The Taxi Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Third Party</li><li>Third party Fire and Theft</li><li>Comprehensive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Taxi insurance encompasses three main market types. Third Party insurance covers damages or injuries to others in an accident where the taxi driver is at fault, but does not cover the driver's vehicle. Third Party Fire and Theft extends this coverage to include protection against fire damage and theft of the taxi. Comprehensive insurance offers the most extensive protection, covering third-party liabilities as well as damage to the taxi itself from accidents, vandalism, and other incidents.</p></p>
