<p><strong>The Compressor Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heavy Hammer Starter</li><li>PTC Starter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compressor starter market primarily features two types: Heavy Hammer Starters and PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) Starters. Heavy Hammer Starters utilize a mechanical hammer mechanism to provide a strong initial torque for large compressors, ideal for high-load applications. PTC Starters, on the other hand, employ thermistor elements to control current and provide smoother motor starts, ensuring energy efficiency and reducing electrical stress. Both starters serve to enhance compressor reliability, minimize wear, and improve operational efficiency in various industrial settings.</p></p>
