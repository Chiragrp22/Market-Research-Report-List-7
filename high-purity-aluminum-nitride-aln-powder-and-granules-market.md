<p><p>The High-purity Aluminum Nitride (AlN) Powder and Granules market features two primary production methods: the Direct Nitridation Method and the Carbothermal Reduction and Nitridation Method. The Direct Nitridation Method involves reacting aluminum with nitrogen at elevated temperatures to produce AlN directly, resulting in high-purity products. The Carbothermal Reduction and Nitridation Method combines carbon with aluminum oxide and subsequently nitrides it, offering control over purity and grain size. Both methods cater to various industrial applications, including electronics and ceramics.</p></p>
