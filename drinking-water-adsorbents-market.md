<p><p>Drinking water adsorbents are materials used to remove contaminants, impurities, and pollutants from water through adsorption, a process where molecules adhere to the surface of the adsorbent material. These materials are effective in eliminating heavy metals, organic compounds, chlorine, and other harmful substances.</p><p>The Drinking Water Adsorbents Market is experiencing steady growth, driven by increasing concerns over water pollution, stringent government regulations, and rising demand for clean drinking water.
