<p><p>The Diode Medical Laser market comprises three main types based on their excitation methods. Electric Injection Lasers generate laser light through electrical currents, making them efficient for various medical applications. Light Pump Lasers utilize external light sources to excite the laser medium, offering versatility in treatments like cosmetic and dermatological procedures. High Energy Electron Beam Excitation Lasers employ accelerated electrons to stimulate lasers, providing high power for specialized medical interventions. Together, these types cater to diverse therapeutic needs in healthcare settings.</p></p>
